Banana Chocolate Almond Milk Smoothie

Description

This lactose-free chocolate almond milk smoothie is made with whey protein powder to give you an extra boost of energy. You can make your own fresh almond milk or pick up a carton at your local grocery store to save time.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup almond milk
  • 1 medium banana
  • 1 ½ ounces dark chocolate bar (such as Hershey's® Special Dark), broken into pieces
  • 1 scoop whey protein powder (Optional)

Steps

  1. Combine almond milk, banana, dark chocolate, and protein powder in a blender; blend until smooth.
