Roberts bread that you may call focaccia but don't when theres italians around

Ingredients

  • 100gr of sourdough starter
  • 300ml water
  • 500gr flour
  • 1 full teaspoon of salt
  • 1 full tablespoon of mixed herbs
  • olive oil

steps

  1. Mix the sourdough starter, the water, salt, herbs and 4 tablespoons of olive oil. I like to use a whisk.
  2. Stir in the flour.
